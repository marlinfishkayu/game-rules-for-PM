Blockchain SicBo Game Rules
Win conditions
  • Blockchain Sicbo follows the standard rules of Sicbo . Its special feature is that the result of the game can be verified through SHA-256 technology to ensure fairness and justice.
  • Blockchain Sicbo is a game guessing the number of points or combinations drawn by 3 dice. Players place their bets in different betting areas on the gaming table. If their content of bet(s) is/are the same as the points or combinations drawn by 3 dice, then they win the game.


Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits respectively.
  3. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  4. After the betting countdown is over, no bet may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  5. Dice will be shaked automatically
  6. After the dice stops rolling, the number of points and combinations on the 3 dice will determine if the player’s bet wins or loses.
  7. The gaming result can be verified after the result is released
