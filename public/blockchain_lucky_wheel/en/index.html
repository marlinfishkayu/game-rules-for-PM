Blockchain Mahjong Lucky Wheel Game Rules
Win conditions
  • Blockchain Mahjong Lucky Wheel follows the standard rules of Mahjong Lucky Wheel . Its special feature is that the result of the game can be verified through SHA-256 technology to ensure fairness and justice.
  • There are 52 equal segments on the wheel. Each segment is represented by a specific symbol (East Wind, South Wind, West Wind, North Wind, White Dragon, Red Dragon, Green Dragon) with its corresponding odds (1, 3, 5, 10, 20, 45, 45) and background-color.
  • In the game, players may choose to place bets on different betting areas. If the clapper points to the symbol which is the same as the player's bet, the player wins.

Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. You may choose to bet on any one or more of the betting areas. The betting options in Mahjong Lucky Wheel are: ‘East Wind’, ‘South Wind’, ‘West Wind’, ‘North Wind’, ‘White Dragon’, ‘Red Dragon’ and ‘Green Dragon’
  3. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits respectively.
  4. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  5. After the betting countdown is over, no bet may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  6. Spins the wheel which must complete at least three revolutions starting from the symbol indicated by the clapper before the wheel is spun.
  7. After the wheel has stopped, the clapper points to the symbol which indicates the result of the game.
  8. The gaming result can be verified after the result is released
