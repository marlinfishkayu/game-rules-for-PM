Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat Game Rules
Win conditions
  • Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat follows the standard rules of Baccarat. Its special feature is that after the betting time is up, you have a right to squeeze the cards if you wager on the 'Player' or 'Banker' and the result of the game can be verified through SHA-256 technology to ensure fairness and justice.
  • Either the ‘Player's hand’ or the ‘Banker's hand’ point value equal to or closest to 9 wins. The suits (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds and Clubs) have no meaning in Baccarat games. 'Kings', 'Queens', 'Jacks' and 'Tens' count zero; 'Aces' counts one and the other cards (2、3、4、5、6、7、8、9) count their corresponding point value.
  • Point value is calculated by summing up the card values and using only the last digit of the total.

Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. The 'Player' and the 'Banker' will be dealt 2 cards at the start of each hand. The cards are dealt in the following order: ‘Player’ > ‘Banker’ > ‘Player' > ‘Banker’.
  3. You may choose to wager on any one or more of the betting areas. There are six betting options in Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat: ‘Player’, ‘Banker’, ‘Tie’, ‘Player Pair’, ‘Banker Pair’ and ‘Super 6’ (‘Super 6’ Only applicable for ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’).
  4. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits.
  5. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  6. After the betting countdown is over, no wager may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  7. You may squeeze the cards if you wagar on the 'Player' or 'Banker'.
  8. After the first round of squeezing time, the system turns the 'Player' cards and 'Banker' cards faceup in sequence. When necessary, additional cards are drawn according to the drawing rules.
  9. If the third card is required, there is a second round squeezing. You may get on the second round of squeezing.
  10. After the cards are opened, the opened cards can be verified

Squeezing card rules
  • You may squeeze the cards if you wager on 'Player' or 'Banker'; You have no right to squeeze the cards if you wager on ‘Tie’, ‘Super 6’, ‘Player Pair’ and ‘Banker Pair’.
  • You may squeeze the ‘Banker’ cards if you wager on ‘Banker’; You may squeeze the ‘Player’ cards if you wager on ‘Player.
  • You may squeeze both hand’s cards if you wager on ‘Banker’ and ‘Player’.
  • After the squeezing time, the system will open electronic play cards automatically.

Payout rules
  • There are two payout rules, including ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ and ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’
    1. Bet on ‘Player’
      • ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ and ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 1 ： 1
      • If the ‘Player's hand’ value is higher than the ‘Banker's hand’ value, any bet on ‘Player’ is paid 1 to 1, and the initial bet is returned;
      • If the ‘Player's hand’ and ‘Banker's hand’ have the same value, any bet on ‘Player’ is returned;
      • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Player’ is removed
    2. Bet on ‘Banker’
    1. ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 0.95 ： 1
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ value is higher than the ‘Player’s hand’ value, any bet on ‘Banker’ is paid 0.95 to 1, and the initial bet is returned;
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ and ‘Player's hand’ values are of the same value, any bet on ‘Banker’ is returned；
      • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Banker’ is removed
    2. ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 1 ： 1 or 0.5 ： 1
      • The House takes no commission, but the payout ratio for ‘Banker’ wins is different as follows,
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ value is higher than the ‘Player's hand’ value and the ‘Banker's hand’ value is not 6, any bet on ‘Banker’ is paid 1 to 1, and the initial bet is returned
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ value is higher than the ‘Player's hand’ value and the ‘Banker's hand’ value is 6, any bet on ‘Banker’ is paid 0.5 to 1, and the initial bet is returned
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ and ‘Player's hand’ values are of the same value, any bet on ‘Banker’ is returned
      • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Banker’ is removed
    1. Bet on ‘Tie’
      • ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ and ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 8 ： 1
      • If the ‘Player's hand’ and ‘Banker's hand’ values are of the same value, any bet on ‘Tie’ is paid 8 to 1, and the initial bet is returned
      • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Tie’ is removed
    2. Bet on ‘Player Pair’
      • ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ and ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 11 ： 1
      • If the ‘Player’ first two cards constitute a pair (only count point, regardless of suits), any bet on 'Player Pair' is paid 11 to 1, and the initial bet is returned.
      • Otherwise, any bet on 'Player Pair' is removed
    3. Bet on ‘Banker Pair’
      • ‘Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ and ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 11 ： 1
      • If the ‘Banker’ first two cards constitute a pair (only count point, regardless of suits), any bet on 'Banker Pair' is paid 11 to 1, and the initial bet is returned
      • Otherwise, any bet on 'Banker Pair' is removed
    4. Bet on ‘Super 6’
      • ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’ - 12 ： 1
      • Only active on ‘No Commission Blockchain Squeeze Baccarat’
      • If the ‘Banker's hand’ value is higher than the ‘Player's hand’ value and the ‘Banker's hand’ value is 6, any bet on 'Super 6' is paid 12 to 1, and the initial bet is returned
      • Otherwise, any bet on 'Super 6' is removed
