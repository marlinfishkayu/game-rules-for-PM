Roulette Game Rules
Win conditions
  • There are 37 numbered pockets on the roulette. There is a green pocket numbered 0 and the other pockets of the roulette are numbered from 1 to 36, with red and black each taking half.
  • In the game, players may choose to place bets on different betting areas, including a single number or a range of numbers etc. If the rolling ball falls into the number pocket which is the same as the player's bet, the player wins.

Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. You may choose to bet on any one or more of the betting areas. The betting options in Roulette are: ‘Big／Small’, ‘Odd／Even’, ‘Red／Black’, 0-36, 1-3 dozen and 1-3 column.
  3. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits respectively.
  4. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  5. After the betting countdown is over, no bet may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  6. The dealer spins the roulette and throws the ball into the roulette. Let the ball spin at least 7 times in the roulette, and finally land in one of the number pockets, which is the result of the game.

Throw rules
  1. We provide a game similar to a real roulette. If there is any special situation, we will handle it in the way which is shown on company notice.
  2. For the following special situations:
    1. If the dealer throws the ball out of the roulette, it will be regarded as the "flying ball".
    2. If the ball rotates less than 7 times, it has fallen into the number pocket.
    3. Ball keeps spinning without falling into the number pocket.
    When the above special situation occurs, the dealer will stop the roulette from spinning and re-throw the ball.
  3. If the roulette stops spinning during the game caused by the dealer due to any reasons, then the ball needs to be re-thrown for that round.
  4. In case of settlement errors, it will be re-settled according to the current video results.
