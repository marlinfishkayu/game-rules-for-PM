God of Fortune SicBo Game Rules
Win conditions
God of Fortune SicBo is a game guessing the total sum of points of all 3 dice. If the bet is the same as the total sum of points drawn by 3 dice, then the player wins the game. Meanwhile, if the total sum of points is the same as the "Lucky Number", the payout will be counted based on the random multiplier given by the "Lucky Number".

Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits respectively.
  3. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  4. After stopping betting, no bet may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  5. After the betting countdown is over, the system will draw one or more "Lucky Number(s)" from the betting area. Each "Lucky Number" will be given a random multiplier (Please refer to "Payout Rules") up to 1000x, which includes the initial bet.
  6. The game host initiates the roll of the dice down the dice tower, and once the dice have stopped tumbling, the total sum of points drawn by all 3 dice will determine if the player's bet wins or loses.
  7. If there is any problem during the game, such as dice stacking up, the dealer will re-roll it again to obtain a new result.
