Blockchain Dragon Tiger Game Rules
Win conditions
  • Blockchain Dragon Tiger follows the standard rules of Dragon Tiger. The result of the game is verified through SHA-256 technology to ensure fairness and justice.
  • Compare two cards, the highest ranking card will win. The suits (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds and Clubs) have no meaning in Blockchain Dragon Tiger.
  • 'Aces' counts one; ‘Jacks’ counts eleven; ‘Queens’ counts twelve; ‘Kings’ counts thirteen; the other cards (2、3、4、5、6、7、8、9、10) count their corresponding point value.

Operation and betting guide
  1. After the new round begins, the betting countdown starts.
  2. The first card will be burnt at the beginning of each game. The cards are dealt in the following order: ‘Dragon’ > ‘Tiger’.
  3. You may choose to wager on one or more betting areas. There are three betting options in Blockchain Dragon Tiger: 'Dragon', 'Tiger', 'Tie'.
  4. Your single bet amount cannot be higher or lower than the upper and lower single bet limits respectively.
  5. If no bet is placed within 5 rounds, the system will return you to the game lobby.
  6. After the betting countdown is over, no wager may be placed, and all bets that have been placed cannot be changed or retrieved.
  7. After 'stop betting', 'Dragon' card and ‘Tiger’ card will be turned face up in sequence.
  8. After the cards are opened, the opened cards can be verified.

Payout rules
  1. Bet on ‘Dragon’ (Payoff: 1:1)
    • If the ‘Dragon’s hand’ value is higher than the ‘Tiger’s hand’ value, any bet on ‘Dragon’ is paid 1 to 1, and the initial bet is returned.
    • If the ‘Dragon’s hand’ and ‘Tiger’s hand’ have the same value, return half bet to the player.*
    • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Dragon’ is removed.
  2. Bet on ‘Tiger’ (Payoff: 1:1)
    • If the ‘Tiger’s hand’ value is higher than the ‘Dragon’s hand’ value, any bet on ‘Tiger’ is paid 1 to 1, and the initial bet is returned.
    • If the ‘Tiger’s hand’ and ‘Dragon’s hand’ have the same value, return half bet to the player.*
    • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Tiger’ is removed.
  3. Bet on ‘Tie’ (Payoff: 8:1)
    • If the ‘Dragon’s hand’ and ‘Tiger’s hand’ have the same value, any bet on ‘Tie’ is paid 8 to 1, and the initial bet is returned.*
    • Otherwise, any bet on ‘Tie’ is removed.
